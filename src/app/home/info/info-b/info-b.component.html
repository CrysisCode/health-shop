RESPONSIBILITY AND QUALITY ARE OUR PRIORITY

As well as having exceptional nutritional qualities, hemp, which we make our hemp oil from, significantly helps lower the emission of harm-ful gases. Our goal is to help both you and the planet.

In producing 1 ton of hemp, we lower the amount of CO2 in the atmosphere by 1.37 tons.

The oil is gently pressed in small amounts whilst cold.

Hemp oil is a ZERO WASTE product- high-quality hemp protein or flour is also produced from the pressing.

We look after its quality from sowing to harvesting. We only use fresh seeds from the most recent crop.
